Days Of The Week

Monday

Workout #1

Workout (3 sets of each)
  • Squats - 15 reps
  • Push-ups - 10-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows - 10 reps per arm
  • Lunges - 10 reps per leg
  • Plank - 30-60 seconds

Tuesday

Workout #2

  • 30 minutes of cardio (running, cycling, or swimming)
  • Ab crunches - 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Leg raises - 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Russian twists - 3 sets of 20 reps
Cool-Down (5-10 minutes)
  • Light stretching

Wednesday

Workout #3

Workout (3 sets of each)
  • Dumbbell bench press - 12 reps
  • Bicep curls - 12 reps
  • Tricep dips - 12 reps
  • Shoulder press - 12 reps
  • Lat pulldowns or pull-ups - 10 reps

Thursday

Workout #4

Workout (3 sets of each)
  • Dumbbell bench press - 12 reps
  • Bicep curls - 12 reps
  • Tricep dips - 12 reps
  • Shoulder press - 12 reps
  • Lat pulldowns or pull-ups - 10 reps

Friday

Workout #5

  • 30 minutes of cardio (running, cycling, or swimming)
  • Ab crunches - 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Leg raises - 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Russian twists - 3 sets of 20 reps
Cool-Down (5-10 minutes)
  • Light stretching
